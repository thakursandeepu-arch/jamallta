Information We Collect

  • Contact details such as name, phone number, email address, and location.
  • Event details such as date, venue, schedule, family contact person, and selected services.
  • Booking and payment records needed to confirm and manage your project.
  • Messages, references, shot preferences, and feedback shared through WhatsApp, email, phone, or forms.
  • Basic website usage data such as browser type, device information, pages visited, and approximate location when analytics tools are active.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to enquiries and prepare quotations for photography, videography, albums, and editing work.
  • To plan shoots, coordinate the team, manage timelines, and deliver final files.
  • To process payments, maintain records, and handle client support.
  • To improve our website, portfolio, communication, and service quality.
  • To send service updates or promotional messages only when appropriate or with your consent.

Photos, Videos, and Creative Material

Wedding and event media can include personal images, voices, locations, family members, guests, rituals, and private moments. We handle this material with care and use it only for editing, delivery, archiving, support, and agreed portfolio use.

If you do not want selected photos or videos used in our website, social media, portfolio, or promotions, please tell us in writing before or after delivery.

Sharing, Storage, and Security

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We may share necessary details with trusted team members, editors, album vendors, delivery partners, payment providers, or technical service providers when required to complete your project.
  • We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect client data and media files.
  • No internet transfer or storage system is completely risk-free, so absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies or third-party analytics to understand traffic, improve pages, and measure performance. You can block or delete cookies through your browser settings. Some website features may work differently if cookies are disabled.

Your Choices and Rights

  • You may request access to personal information we hold about you.
  • You may ask us to correct inaccurate details.
  • You may request deletion of personal information, subject to legal, payment, project, or record-keeping requirements.
  • You may opt out of promotional messages at any time.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page with the updated date.
