Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 23, 2026
At Jamallta Films, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone, and address when you contact or book with us.
  • Event Details: Wedding/event dates, venues, preferences.
  • Payment Data: Billing details and transaction records (processed securely).
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and access times via cookies and analytics tools.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and deliver our photography and videography services.
  • To communicate with you regarding bookings, quotations, and service updates.
  • To process payments and manage accounts.
  • To improve our website, services, and customer experience.
  • To send promotional emails about new services, offers, and events (with your consent).

Cookies & Tracking

  • We use cookies to enhance user experience and analyze website traffic.
  • You can control cookie settings through your browser. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.
  • Third-party analytics (e.g., Google Analytics) may collect anonymized usage data.

Data Security

  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data.
  • Data is stored on secure servers with access controls.
  • While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

User Rights

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Opt-Out: You may unsubscribe from promotional communications at any time.

Updates to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
